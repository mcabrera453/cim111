DREAM: A Silent Film

My silent film is entitled "DREAM". It follows a girl through her subconscious mind. The goal of the project was to tell the story of a dream without using words. I decided to use a projector to display the characters dream over her. This was an experimental approach that brought the visuals of a dream state into a simple room.
